Product Overview

What is Role Tree?

Role Tree is a Salesforce‑native app that visualizes the UserRole hierarchy and simplifies moving users and roles in complex org structures. It respects Salesforce permissions throughout.

  • Read‑only view for non‑admins
  • Full management for authorized administrators
  • Fast search and node selection
  • Minimal clicks to reorganize teams

Who is it for?

Designed for Salesforce Administrators, Architects, and Security teams in mid‑to‑large orgs.
